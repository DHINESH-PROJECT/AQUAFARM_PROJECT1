Fish Species Directory

Comprehensive guide to fish species and their care requirements

Atlantic Salmon

Salmo salar

Optimal Temp: 12-18°C pH: 6.0-8.0 Feed: 3x daily

Atlantic salmon are anadromous fish that are prized for their high-quality meat and adaptability to various farming conditions.

Rainbow Trout

Oncorhynchus mykiss

Optimal Temp: 10-16°C pH: 6.5-8.5 Feed: 4x daily

Rainbow trout are hardy fish that grow quickly and are excellent for both commercial and recreational farming.

Tilapia

Oreochromis niloticus

Optimal Temp: 20-30°C pH: 6.5-8.5 Feed: 2x daily

Tilapia are tropical fish known for their fast growth rate and tolerance to various water conditions.

Channel Catfish

Ictalurus punctatus

Optimal Temp: 24-29°C pH: 6.5-8.5 Feed: 2x daily

Channel catfish are bottom-dwelling fish that are highly adaptable and have excellent feed conversion rates.

Common Carp

Cyprinus carpio

Optimal Temp: 18-24°C pH: 6.5-8.0 Feed: 3x daily

Common carp are hardy fish that can tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions and are popular in aquaculture.

Striped Bass

Morone saxatilis

Optimal Temp: 15-25°C pH: 7.0-8.5 Feed: 3x daily

Striped bass are prized game fish that require specific conditions but offer high market value when successfully farmed.
